About us

Pro Consult is professional consulting from specialists in their fields to solve your everyday problems.

As it happens, you have some everyday task and you can't solve it, find answers to your questions, turn to charlatans and time, the way to solve your problem is getting bigger and wider, and your wallet is getting thinner. This is what our site is for. There are only quality experts in their fields with proper education and extensive experience.

We invite specialists from Latvia and other countries such as Russia, Estonia and Lithuania to our site. Our specialists can offer you qualitative consultations in the following fields: automobile issues (car market, car repair, selection), IT development (the whole range of services, front and back), trade (small and medium business), real estate (all questions concerning real estate transactions, selling/buying/renting), medicine (recommendations about right treatment, consultations of people who were able to cure various diseases) and many others.

Before ordering a consultation, you need to contact us and tell us your problem or exactly what kind of consultation you need. After getting preliminary information, we can tell you which specialist is more suitable for you and you can order his consultation on our website. You can make a consultation via Whatsapp/viber/telegram, or you can order a personal consultation, it will take place in our office.

We work with both individuals and companies.

If you have any questions, all our contacts are on the site.
